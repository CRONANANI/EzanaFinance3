Congressional Trading

We aggregate and analyze publicly disclosed congressional stock trades from the STOCK Act filings. Our platform:

  • Monitors all 535 members of Congress (House and Senate)
  • Updates in real-time as new disclosures are filed
  • Provides advanced filtering by politician, party, chamber, stock, and trade type
  • Sends instant alerts when followed politicians make trades
  • Analyzes trading patterns and correlations with committee assignments

Our data is updated in real-time as congressional members file their STOCK Act disclosures. By law, members must report trades within 45 days of the transaction date. We process these filings immediately upon public availability, typically within minutes of disclosure.

Pro and Family subscribers receive instant push notifications for trades from followed politicians.

Market Intelligence

We provide comprehensive market intelligence across four key areas:

  • Government Contracts: Track contract awards by agency, contractor, and dollar amount
  • Lobbying Activity: Monitor lobbying expenditures and correlate with stock performance
  • Patent Filings: Analyze innovation trends through patent velocity and citations
  • Insider Trading: Track corporate insider buying and selling activity

All data is aggregated from official government sources and updated in real-time.

Government contracts often signal future revenue streams and can be leading indicators for stock performance. Our platform helps you:

  • Identify companies winning large contracts before market awareness
  • Track contract trends in specific sectors (defense, healthcare, tech)
  • Analyze historical correlations between contract awards and stock performance
  • Set alerts for contract awards to companies in your watchlist

For example, a major defense contractor winning a $500M contract might signal revenue growth for the next 3-5 years.

Patent momentum measures the rate and quality of patent filings by companies. It's valuable because:

  • Innovation indicator: High patent velocity often precedes new product launches
  • Competitive moats: Patent portfolios can create barriers to entry
  • M&A targets: Companies with strong patent portfolios are often acquisition targets
  • Technology trends: Identify emerging technologies before they go mainstream

We track over 100,000 patents annually across technology, pharmaceutical, and industrial sectors.

Data & Security

Security is our top priority. We employ multiple layers of protection:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Authentication: JWT-based authentication with bcrypt password hashing
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on enterprise-grade Azure cloud infrastructure
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II certified with annual audits
  • Access control: Role-based permissions and multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and anomaly detection

We never sell or share your personal financial data with third parties.

We source data from verified, official sources:

  • Congressional trades: House and Senate STOCK Act disclosure databases
  • Government contracts: USAspending.gov and agency contract databases
  • Lobbying data: Senate Lobbying Disclosure Database
  • Patent information: USPTO (United States Patent and Trademark Office)
  • Market data: Licensed feeds from major exchanges and data providers
  • Company fundamentals: SEC EDGAR filings and financial data providers

All data is verified, normalized, and quality-checked before being made available.

Congressional Trading Data

Data Overview

Ezana Finance provides comprehensive congressional trading data powered by Quiver Quantitative.

Available Data Points

  • Politician Name: Full name of the congress member
  • Ticker Symbol: Stock or asset ticker
  • Transaction Type: Purchase, Sale, or Exchange
  • Amount Range: Disclosed dollar range of transaction
  • Trade Date: Date the transaction occurred
  • Disclosure Date: Date the transaction was reported
  • Chamber: House of Representatives or Senate
  • Party: Democrat, Republican, or Independent
  • Asset Description: Full description of the security
  • Committee Assignments: Relevant committee positions

Data Source

All congressional trading data is sourced from official STOCK Act disclosures and aggregated by Quiver Quantitative, a leading provider of alternative financial data.

Data is updated in real-time as new disclosures are filed and is verified for accuracy before being made available on the platform.

Filtering & Analysis

Our platform allows you to:

  • Filter by politician, party, chamber, and transaction type
  • Track specific congress members with follow system
  • Receive real-time alerts for new trades
  • Analyze trading patterns and correlations
  • View historical trading activity
